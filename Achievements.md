# Tic Tux Toe — Achievements (Categorized)

This file reorganizes the 100 Steam achievements into categories to make them easier to browse and assign in Steamworks.

**Basics**
- **1. First Move**: Place your first mark on the board.
- **2. First Win**: Win your first match.
- **3. First Draw**: Play to a draw for the first time.
- **4. Tux Apprenticeship**: Complete 5 matches.
- **5. Tux Journeyman**: Complete 20 matches.
- **6. Tux Master**: Complete 50 matches.

**Openings & Tactics**
- **7. Perfect Start**: Win after playing the center first.
- **8. Edge Mastery**: Win after starting on an edge.
- **9. Corner King**: Win after starting in a corner.
- **10. Quick Victory**: Win within three moves.
- **11. Defensive Wall**: Force a draw from a losing position.
- **12. Comeback Kid**: Win after your opponent had two in a row.
- **15. Strategist**: Win a match without giving your opponent a chance.
- **16. Clean Sheet**: Win without your opponent placing any marks in the center.
- **17. Fork Finder**: Create a winning fork.
- **18. Fork Blocker**: Block an opponent's fork successfully.
- **19. Double Threat**: Win with two simultaneous winning lines.
- **20. Last Move Hero**: Win on the final available cell.

**match Flow & Goals**
- **21. Stalemate Specialist**: Record ten draws.
- **22. Patience**: Play a single long match (all moves placed).
- **23. Rapid Fire**: Play three matches in under two minutes.
- **24. Marathon**: Play 100 matches.
- **25. Tux Collector**: Unlock 10 achievements.
- **26. Tux Enthusiast**: Unlock 25 achievements.
- **27. Tux Aficionado**: Unlock 50 achievements.
- **28. Tux Legend**: Unlock 75 achievements.
- **29. Completionist**: Unlock all achievements.

**Losses, Rematches & Learning**
- **30. First Defeat**: Lose your first match.
- **31. Learning Loss**: Lose ten matches.
- **32. Rematch**: Request a rematch after losing.
- **33. Merciful**: Offer a draw that is accepted.
- **34. Aggressive Play**: Start ten matches with the same opening.
- **35. Mix It Up**: Use every possible starting cell at least once.
- **36. Tux Explorer**: Start a match from every corner and edge.

**Player Styles & Variations**
- **37. Predictable**: Win against an opponent who repeats moves.
- **38. Unpredictable**: Win using a rarely used opening.
- **39. Headhunter**: Win against an opponent ranked higher than you.
- **40. David vs Goliath**: Beat an opponent with triple your win count.
- **41. Silent Strategist**: Win without chatting in a multiplayer match.
- **42. Chatty Tux**: Send 50 messages in multiplayer.

**Multiplayer & Community**
- **43. Friendly Rival**: Play 25 different opponents.
- **44. Local Champ**: Win a local multiplayer match.
- **45. Online Rookie**: Win your first online match.
- **46. Online Competitor**: Reach 10 online wins.
- **47. Online Veteran**: Reach 100 online wins.
- **63. Rematch King**: Win five rematches in a row.
- **64. Underdog**: Win after being behind on the scoreboard.
- **65. Caretaker**: Offer draws to weaker players three times.
- **70. Teaching Moment**: Win while helping a newcomer learn.
- **71. Team Player**: Win a cooperative or team variant match.
- **85. Polite Player**: Receive a 'well played' from an opponent.
- **86. Sportsmanship**: Give a GG or GL in chat after a match.
- **99. Community Helper**: Post a strategy guide or tip accepted by the community.

**AI & Single-Player**
- **48. AI Tamer**: Beat the hardest AI difficulty.
- **49. AI Apprentice**: Beat the easy AI ten times.
- **50. AI Grinder**: Beat medium AI 25 times.
- **96. Legacy**: Have an AI learn from your playstyle (if supported).

**Timed & Competitive Play**
- **51. No Mercy**: Win without conceding any moves in a timed match.
- **52. Timed Champion**: Win a timed match on sudden death.
- **53. Blitz Master**: Win three blitz matches in a row.
- **54. Unflappable**: Win after losing the previous match.

**Tactics, Tricks & Mastery**
- **55. Mirror Match**: Win when both players mirror moves early.
- **56. Sacrificial Lamb**: Sacrifice a winning line to force a draw.
- **57. Puzzle Solver**: Solve a daily puzzle challenge.
- **72. Combo Artist**: Chain three strategic blocks in one match.
- **73. Zen Master**: Play a match with no mistakes.
- **74. Perfectionist**: Win ten matches without mistakes.
- **79. Veteran Moves**: Use advanced tactics successfully five times.
- **91. Combo Collector**: Perform every known opening tactic once.
- **83. Mind Reader**: Block an opponent's obvious win twice in one match.
- **84. Switcheroo**: Win after switching sides mid-match.

**Replays, Puzzles & Content**
- **57. Puzzle Solver**: Solve a daily puzzle challenge.
- **58. Daily Regular**: Play seven daily puzzles in a row.
- **59. Weekly Streak**: Play daily puzzles for four consecutive weeks.
- **60. Tux Historian**: View 50 replays.
- **61. Rewind**: Watch a full match replay from start to finish.
- **62. Highlight Reel**: Save five replays.

**Progression & Records**
- **13. Unstoppable**: Win five matches in a row.
- **14. Invincible**: Win ten matches in a row.
- **67. Pattern Breaker**: Break a ten-match losing streak.
- **68. Lucky Tux**: Win a match with low probability (random outcome).
- **69. Calculated Risk**: Win after an intentional sacrifice.
- **80. Unbroken**: Never lose for 25 consecutive matches.
- **81. Comeback Streak**: Win three comeback matches consecutively.

**Modes, Customization & Creation**
- **75. Tux Fashionista**: Change your avatar or theme five times.
- **89. Trailblazer**: Try a newly released match mode.
- **90. Mode Collector**: Play all available match modes.
- **92. No Holds Barred**: Play a match with all settings maxed.
- **97. Custom Champ**: Win a match using custom rules.

**Specials & Legacy**

- **77. Hidden Finder**: Discover ten secrets or hidden tips.
- **95. Echoes of Victory**: Win a rematch after an initial loss.
- **88. Local Legend**: Win a tournament or local ladder.
- **24. Marathon**: Play 100 matches.
- **87. Seasoned Rival**: Play 200 matches.
- **100. Tux Eternal**: Be remembered on the match's Hall of Fame.

Enjoy unlocking them all — good luck, Tux player!
